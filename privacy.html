Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Magic Domino™ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Magic Domino collects minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Game Data: Your scores, game history, and tournament results are stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud Sync (Premium): If you enable iCloud sync, your game data is stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.
  • Camera Access: Used only for AI score counting. Photos are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded or stored.
  • Purchase Information: Managed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not store your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality (score tracking, AI counting, tournaments)
  • To sync your data across devices via iCloud (if enabled)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage and Security

All your game data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's secure infrastructure. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

Magic Domino may use the following services:

  • Apple App Store: For purchases and subscriptions
  • iCloud: For optional data sync (Premium feature)
  • Ad Networks: For free users, ads are served by third-party networks. Premium users have an ad-free experience.

Children's Privacy

Magic Domino does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly and safe for all ages.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your game data at any time from within the app
  • Disable iCloud sync
  • Request deletion of your account and data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
